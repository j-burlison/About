The application...

kept track of on call incedent response teams and their members An Incedent Manager could select a team member and assign them to the current incendent The application would send out an invitation email to said team member The team member could then reply to that email to indicate their acceptance to the invitation After the invitation is sent out, the team member was thrown into a running que that kept track of all invited team members and their current status within the incident (invited, accepted, joined, dismissed, re-joined ect.) This running que was visible to the Incident manager via thee ui Each incident is represented in a DB form that held all of the Incident's metrics The queue and all of its activity was saved to an Icident Resonse DB form for later analitics
